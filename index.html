How it works

Three steps. Zero paperwork.

From the moment a patient walks in to their prescription — handled.

01

Connect your clinic

Link your Gmail once, and appointments from Practo, Apollo, 1mg, and walk-ins flow into one schedule automatically. No manual data entry.

02

Just speak during the consult

Tap Record and talk normally. MangoMD transcribes in real time and generates SOAP notes + a prescription in under 30 seconds. No typing mid-consultation.

03

Review, save, done

Glance at the AI-generated prescription, make any edits, and save. Filed to the patient's timeline instantly.
